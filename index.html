About Us

Islamophobia is a dangerous phenomenon that creates a hostile environment for Muslims and fuels negative attitudes towards Islam. It can lead to the marginalization of Muslim communities and limit their opportunities for social and economic advancement. It has especially been an issue in the western media and has created many hate crimes to people due to their religion and place of birth. It perpetuates negative stereotypes and undermines the values of diversity and inclusivity in society.
